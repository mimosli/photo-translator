The Book

Your & My Life.
In Poems.

my roller-coaster life is not a book you simply read. It is one that lets you recognise yourself. Page by page you encounter the unspeakable — the nights that lasted too long, the love that was too big, the freedom you fight for every day.

leila frey writes as if she has read your thoughts. Because she had the same ones.

The Author

leila frey

leila frey writes for everyone who has ever felt too loud for a world that expects silence.

my roller-coaster life takes you on a journey — starting in Black: the free fall, the heartbreak, the self-destruction. Then comes Red and Pink — the adventure, the desire, the love that burns. And finally Green and White: hope, strength, friendship — the light that stays.

She writes with radical honesty about what we all feel but rarely say out loud — so you know: you are not alone in this.
